About Dr. Monisha

Dr. Monisha S completed her undergraduate medical education at Chettinad Hospital and Research Institute, followed by her MD in Psychiatry from the world-renowned National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bangalore — one of India's most prestigious centres for mental health.

She subsequently pursued a Fellowship in Addiction Psychiatry from NIMHANS, bringing specialised expertise in substance use disorders. Her clinical interests include nicotine addiction, with focused training in alcohol, opioid, and mobile deaddiction.

Dr. Monisha is well-versed in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), having attended multiple workshops to refine her therapeutic approach. She brings warmth, evidence-based care, and deep empathy to every consultation.
