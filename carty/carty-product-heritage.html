The Heritage Automatic

Timeless Precision

$890.00

Rooted in the golden era of Swiss horology, the Heritage Automatic distills a century of watchmaking craft into a single, understated form. Its sunburst silver dial breathes under broad cathedral hands — a quiet declaration of permanence.

The exhibition caseback reveals a decorated rotor engraved with the Carty insignia, spinning with every motion of the wrist. Paired with a hand-stitched dark tan calfskin strap for a finish that only improves with age.

Case Size 38mm
Water Resistance 5 ATM
Power Reserve 48 Hours
Movement Cal. 2824
