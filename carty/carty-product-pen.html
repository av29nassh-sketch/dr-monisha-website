Gold Nib Fountain Pen

The Art of Writing

$495.00

Handcrafted in a three-generation workshop in Heidelberg, this fountain pen redefines what writing can feel like. The body is turned from a single billet of matte-black resin, capped with 18-karat gold plating on the trim and a two-tone 18K gold nib.

The nib is hand-tuned to a medium-fine width and ships with a converter and a 50ml glass bottle of Carty Iron-Gall ink — a dark, water-resistant formula that rewards the deliberate writer.
